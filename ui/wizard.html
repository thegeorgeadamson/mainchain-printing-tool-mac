Welcome to Mainchain Printing Tool

This app lets you print directly from Freman on your Mac. It runs in your menu bar and connects your macOS printers to Mainchain's freight management system.

Let's get you set up in a few quick steps.

Your Printers

These printers were detected on your Mac. They'll be available in Freman for printing consignment notes, labels, and declarations.

Connect to Freman

Enter this URL in your Freman printing settings to connect.

http://localhost:50515
Port: Default: 50515
  1. Open Freman in your browser
  2. Go to Maintain > Settings > Printing
  3. Paste the URL into Mainchain Printing Tool Link
  4. Click Test connection to verify
  5. Assign printers to each print type and click Save
✅

You're All Set!

The printer icon is now in your menu bar. Click it anytime to see your printers and recent print jobs.
